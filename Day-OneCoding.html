Check below on How to make a Tea:

Tea Infuser: A tea infuser keeps any tea leaves from floating freely in your cup while you drink. Teapot or Tea Mug. A teapot or tea mug is what you place the infuser in to brew tea. Tea Kettle. You will need a tea kettle to boil water. Loose Leaf Tea.

Ingredients

  • Flower
  • Water
  • Ginger
  • Clove

Preparation

  1. Fill up the kettle with water
  2. Boil the Kettle.
  3. Place a teabag in your favourite mug.
  4. Pour boiling water into your favorite mug.
